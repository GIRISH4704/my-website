Social Media Optimization (SMO) for Healthcare

A short description of what this service is and who it is for.

What this involves

In today’s digital world, patients don’t just search on Google — they also trust what they see on social media. We help your hospital or clinic build a strong, professional presence across platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube, so patients can connect with you even before they visit. Our healthcare-focused SMO strategies are designed to build trust, educate patients, and showcase your expertise. From creating engaging posts and patient success stories to managing reviews and increasing followers, we make sure your brand stands out and stays active.

What you get

  • Build a strong and trustworthy online presence
  • Share patient testimonials & treatment awareness content
  • Increase engagement with potential patients
  • Consistent posting & creative healthcare designs

Online Reputation Management (ORM) for Healthcare

When it comes to choosing a doctor or hospital, patients heavily rely on online reviews and ratings. A single negative review without a proper response can impact your credibility. We help you actively monitor, manage, and enhance your online reputation across platforms like Google, Practo, and social media. Our healthcare-specific ORM strategies ensure that your brand reflects trust, professionalism, and patient satisfaction. From responding to patient feedback to highlighting positive experiences, we help you maintain a strong and reliable digital image.

What you get

  • Monitor and manage patient reviews across platforms
  • Respond professionally to both positive & negative feedback
  • Improve ratings and build patient trust
  • Highlight positive patient experiences
